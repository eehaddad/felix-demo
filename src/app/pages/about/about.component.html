Who we are

Dr. Jonathan Watson

Johnathan C. Watson Psy.D. is a Licensed Psychologist (#31376) in the state of California. Dr. Watson was born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana where he attended Dillard University to study Psychology. After receiving his bachelor's degree from Dillard University in 2013, he moved to Los Angeles to further pursue a career in psychology. By age 25, Dr. Watson earned his master's and Doctor of Psychology (Psy.D.) degree in Clinical Forensic Psychology from the California School of Forensic Studies (CSFS) at Alliant International University. He completed a Doctoral Internship and Postdoctoral Fellowship conducting intensive treatment and evaluations to Sexually Violent Persons (SVPs) in the state of Illinois.

During this time, he also worked in academia as a collegial Psychology Instructor. Dr. Watson has training in outpatient, inpatient, correctional, and community health settings with individuals who present with numerous mental health issues including depression, mood disorders, psychotic disorders, ADHD, anxiety, trauma, and other neurodevelopmental disorders. Dr. Watson also has a specialization which pertains to treatment and evaluation of sexual offenders.

He is currently an Independent Level Treatment Provider under the California Sex Offender Management Board (CASOMB) and serves as one of nine Certified STABLE- 2007 (dynamic sexual risk assessment) Trainers throughout the state of California.

Dr. Felix Sanchez

Felix Sanchez, Psy.D. is a Licensed Psychologist in California (#30964). Dr. Sanchez earned his doctorate in Clinical-Forensic Psychology from the California School of Forensic Studies (CSFS) at Alliant International University. He has training in outpatient, inpatient, correctional, and community mental health settings with individuals experiencing numerous issues, including depression, bipolar disorder, ADHD, anxiety, trauma, and perceptual difficulties. His research interests include malingering in neuropsychological assessments and juvenile/adult competency to stand trial.

Forensic Evaluations

Dr. Sanchez conducts evaluations in a range of criminal and administrative matters, including but not limited to: (I) Criminal: Competency to Stand Trial and Pre-Trial and Sentencing: Risk Assessment, Diversion, and Mitigation and (II) Administrative/Other: Diagnostic/Treatment Evaluations and Violence Risk Assessment

Expert Testimony and Consultation

Dr. Sanchez provides expert testimony in forensic cases as well as consultation to attorneys, clinicians, and other interested parties.

Clinical Evaluation

Dr. Sanchez also conducts clinical psychological assessments, which may include diagnostic evaluation, assessment of personality and intellectual/cognitive functioning, and additional types of testing to assist in treatment planning and decision-making.

Therapy

Dr. Sanchez works with individuals from various age groups and ethnic, cultural, and socioeconomic backgrounds. He uses an eclectic and varied approach to individual therapy, which is adapted to his client's needs.

Bilingual Services (Spanish)

Dr. Sanchez is fluent in the Spanish language, and he conducts therapy and assessments with Spanish-speaking clients. He also provides consultation services to mental health professionals and agencies as well as to legal professionals who are in need of such services from a bilingual psychologist.
